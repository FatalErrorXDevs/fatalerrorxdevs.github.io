VR Project KLM

A VR Door training replacement for KLM

Together with a team of interdisciplinary students, we came up with a solution for the KLM that can replace their physical doortrainers, which is cheaper and can train many more scenarios than they can do with their solution right now.

I mostly worked on creating the scenario and I made a few 3d models for the tutorial section. I also did research on the physical interaction with real world objects in VR.
